    Functional Connectivity: Computing¶

    In this section of the tutorials, we assume that your data are nicely organised according to the first section of the tutorials, and you’ve fetched the data with the conpagnon.data_handling.data_architecture.fetch_data() function. Next steps involve the extraction of clean brain signals and the computing of the connectivity matrices.

    Important

    ConPagnon extract the brain signals in each region from a brain atlas only. If you don’t have one, you can fetch a atlas among the numerous examples in the Nilearn dataset module !
